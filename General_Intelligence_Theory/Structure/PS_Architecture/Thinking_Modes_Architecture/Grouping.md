# THINKING MODE GROUPING ARCHITECTURE

==================================================
I. MAIN GROUPING (BY DOMAIN OF OPERATION)
==================================================

A. Object-Level Thinking Modes
   → Operate on problem structure
   → Transform variables, constraints, models, hypotheses

B. Control-Level Thinking Modes
   → Operate on cognition itself
   → Regulate reasoning, learning, confidence, attention

C. Hybrid Thinking Modes
   → Operate on both problem structure and cognitive control


==================================================
II. OBJECT-LEVEL THINKING MODES
==================================================

### 1. Exploration Modes
- Lateral Thinking
- Divergent Thinking

### 2. Evaluation Modes
- Critical Thinking
- Convergent Thinking

### 3. Decomposition Modes
- Analytical Thinking

### 4. Structural Manipulation Modes
- Structured Thinking
- Abstraction (Compression)

### 5. Uncertainty Handling Modes
- Probabilistic Thinking

### 6. Strategy & Planning Modes
- Strategic Thinking

### 7. Pattern Transfer Modes
- Analogous Thinking

### 8. Perspective Transformation Modes
- Perspective Thinking


==================================================
III. CONTROL-LEVEL THINKING MODES
==================================================

### 1. Awareness & Monitoring Modes
- Meta-Cognitive Thinking

### 2. Learning & Updating Modes
- Reflective Thinking
- Reinforcement Thinking

### 3. Speed / Heuristic Processing Modes
- Intuitive Thinking


==================================================
IV. HYBRID THINKING MODES (Optional Category)
==================================================

(Some modes may span both object and control levels depending on context)

- Strategic Thinking (planning + cognitive resource control)
- Perspective Thinking (representation + attention shift)
- Intuitive Thinking (fast cognition + pattern recognition)
